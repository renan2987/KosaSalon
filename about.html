Our Story

Ten years of

Kosa is a neighborhood studio on Steinway Street, built on the idea that the best hair decisions are made together — slowly, and in good light.

N°01 How it started

Founded on the consultation.

After years behind the chair in Manhattan, our founder opened Kosa in Astoria to slow things down — to give each guest the diagnosis and honesty that rushed salons skip. The name means hair, and treating it as living material has guided us ever since.

Today our team blends editorial technique with genuine neighborhood warmth. Whether you’re after a discreet grey blend or a head-turning color transformation, the process starts the same way: we ask, we look, we plan.
